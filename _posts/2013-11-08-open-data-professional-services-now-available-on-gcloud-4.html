We are offering a range of services via G-Cloud including setting up and deploying a CKAN open data portal – perfect if you want to start publishing open data quickly.We can also offer technical consultancy and support if you need bespoke features developed for your open data portal.Our full list of services available via G-Cloud 4 are: Set-up and deployment of a CKAN open data portal CKAN – maintenance/support and technical consulting Vendor-neutral Open Data consulting Vendor-neutral Open Data training course for managers If you have any questions or would like to work with us via G-Cloud please get in touch on services@okfn.org.
