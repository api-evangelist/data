Following the International Comparison Program (ICP) 2011 final report release from last October, there was particular interest in the charts presenting the results.To give a deeper explanation of one of the most popular charts, we’ve recently produced this video: Source:  ICP, http://icp.worldbank.org/ The chart summarizes the structure of the world economy and the distribution of its population based on ICP 2011 results.It incorporates three dimensions for each economy: population share, real GDP per capita, and share of world total real GDP.
