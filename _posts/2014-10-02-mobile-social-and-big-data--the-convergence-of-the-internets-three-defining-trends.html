The world is swimming in data.  Smartphone-based computing had led to much more internet-based activity, and more opportunities to collect location data.The consumer stampede into social media means that the tastes, preferences, and frustrations of billions are shared online.  In a couple of recent reports from BI Intelligence, we take stock of how all this data is the bedrock for a new generation of business tactics and applications.We focus on dispelling hype around big data, and describe clearly what it is — and what it isnt.
