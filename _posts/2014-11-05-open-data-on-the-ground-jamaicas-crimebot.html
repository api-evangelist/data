Some areas of Jamaica, particularly major cities such as Kingston and Montego Bay, experience high levels of crime and violence.If you were to type, “What is Jamaica’s biggest problem” in a Google search, you’ll see that the first five results are about crime.Using data to pinpoint high crime areas CrimeBot (www.crimebot.net) fights crime by providing crime hotspot views and sending out alerts based on locations through mobile devices.
